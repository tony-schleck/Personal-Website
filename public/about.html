About Tony

Who I Am

Hi, I’m Tony Schleck, a data-driven professional with a passion for analytics, logistics, and problem-solving. I’ve worked in roles where data wasn’t just numbers — it was the story behind operations, performance, and efficiency.

My Journey

I was born and raised in Edina, Minnesota.

During high school I participated in Model UN, Varsity Lacrosse, Junior Gold Ice Hockey, and was a "SMATH" (Science & Math) peer tutor.

After Minnesota, I began undergraduate studies at the University of Wisconsin - Madison (Go Badgers!) where I earned my Bachelor's of Science in Mathematics and Data Scicence.

My favorite expirience at UW Madison was tutoring for the athletic department. There I met and tutored student athletes who were incredibly greatful for my time and effort.

Shortly after graduation, I began working at Christensen Associates as a Data Analyst on the Postal team.
